I haven't been losing any clients/fans, so why should I worry about optimising my website?

First of all, if not losing clients is your metric, it's a pretty shitty business plan. As an artist or a small business, you should always be trying to attract new fans and clients, preferably without losing existing ones. If your website is old, badly designed or not mobile-friendly then keeping those potential customers for more than 30 seconds is going to be really hard. Optimising your website can help attract new clients, keep them engaged and eventually convert them into paying customers.
